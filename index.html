About Us

Aryauday Foundation is a registered non-profit committed to transforming lives through education, humanitarian aid, and animal welfare. We believe every individual deserves dignity and every child deserves quality learning.

Our Vision

A society where no one is left behind—where education is accessible, basic needs are met, and all beings are treated with compassion.

Our Mission

To provide free quality education, support the needy with food and essentials, and protect and care for cows through our Gau Raksha initiative.

Our Focus Areas

  • Free study classes and digital literacy for underprivileged students
  • Food distribution, clothing drives, and emergency assistance
  • Gau Raksha—shelter, medical care, and welfare for cows

Our Initiatives

Three pillars of change: Education, Humanity, and Gau Mata.

Free Education Program

  • Free study classes for underprivileged students
  • Digital literacy and access support
  • Helping students without quality education due to lack of resources

Helping the Needy

  • Food distribution drives
  • Clothes donation campaigns
  • Medical assistance and emergency help

Gau Raksha Initiative

  • Cow protection and welfare
  • Shelter and care for abandoned cows
  • Medical care for injured and sick cows
